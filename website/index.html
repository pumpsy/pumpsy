Pumpsy Store GitHub

The pump board for your inflatable needs. Work in progress...

Long story short: inflatables need to be inflated and they better do not explode. Pumpsy is a smart pump that makes it possible. It can inflate, deflate and features an on-board interface to program it to do spequences of actions that match your project needs.

Pumpsy is still in development, but in order to get it in you hands, we ask for you support first. Below is a list of what you can do.

  • Visit our store and buy our merch.
    Each item sold contributes to more time spent on prototyping!
  • Documenting the use of Pumpsy in interesting projects.
    It would be great to showcase the use of pumpsy in different inflatable proects. Reach out to us and we will discuss the details!
  • Customization requests.
    If your project needs specific configuration, we can adjust Pumpsy to your specific project needs. Get in touch with us and let's talk about it.

The goal of Pumpsy is to become a little box that you can purchase with one click. Before that, we need some encouragement from you to make it happen!
